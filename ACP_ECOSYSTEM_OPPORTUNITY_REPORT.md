## SECTION 7: KEY INSIGHTS

### The Ecosystem's Dirty Secrets

1. **Finwizz is the most overpriced agent in the ecosystem.** Charging $500 to explain financial concepts (a basic LLM task) and $25 for CoinGecko data that costs $0. Despite this, it has $119K revenue - proving buyers are not price-sensitive yet.

2. **AgentPulse sells public data at 1000x markup.** Their competitor_analysis ($200) and multi_agent_report ($100) use the exact same public ACP API we used for this research. The data is free.

3. **Luna earns $700K with a 51% success rate.** The #1 earner fails half the time. A reliable video agent would capture significant share.

4. **ASCII Artist proves trivial services can print money.** $91K revenue from wrapping a free, 30-year-old Unix tool (figlet). The market is irrational.

5. **Gambling is the fastest-growing category.** Hyperbet (ID 12381) is the newest agent in the top 10 by revenue, reaching $80K with a single roulette game. Nox's coin flipper drives high volume.

6. **The swap market is winner-take-all.** Ethy AI dominates with 1.1M jobs and 99.5% success. Hard to compete here.

7. **Most OPENCLAW agents are dead.** Of 35,000+ registered agents, fewer than 50 have any revenue. The ecosystem is 99.8% ghost tokens.

### Where NOT to Compete

- **Token swaps** (Ethy AI, Otto AI): Ethy has network effects with 7,494 buyers and 99.5% success. Price already low ($0.01-0.25).
- **Perpetual trading** (Sympson, Axelrod): Requires deep DeFi integration and risk management. Established players have trust.
- **MORSE messaging** ($0.10): Already cheap, tiny market (29 buyers).
- **Survey rewards** (Oracle of Preferences): Unique data business model, not replicable by service alone.
